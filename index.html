Our Motive!

Introducing Upeksha Foundation, a vibrant and influential NGO based in the tri-city area, dedicated to making a positive impact in our community and beyond. Upeksha Foundation is driven by a clear vision and a committed team of enthusiastic people who work together to tackle the many problems our society faces. The foundation focuses on creating lasting change in the lives of those in need, addressing issues such as poverty, education, health, and social inequality. By launching innovative projects and showing unwavering dedication, Upeksha Foundation aims to empower individuals, uplift communities, and promote sustainable development. The foundation embodies the spirit of compassion and service, working tirelessly to build a better future for everyone.

Our Past Events

The Upeksha Foundation recently held a successful winter clothing drive to support those in need within the community. By collecting and distributing warm clothing, the foundation aimed to provide comfort and protection during the harsh winter months. Thanks to the generous contributions from local residents and volunteers, Upeksha was able to supply coats, scarves, gloves, and other essential winter wear to hundreds of individuals and families.

The Upeksha Foundation recently organized a summer clothing drive to help those in need during the scorching heat in India. By collecting and distributing lightweight and breathable clothing, the foundation aimed to provide relief and comfort in the intense summer weather. With generous donations from the community and the dedicated efforts of volunteers, Upeksha was able to supply T-shirts, shorts, hats, and other essential summer wear to hundreds of individuals and families. This initiative not only met immediate needs but also showcased the community's spirit of compassion and support.

The Upeksha Foundation recently organized a cattle feeding drive to support local farmers and their livestock during challenging times. By collecting and distributing nutritious feed, the foundation aimed to ensure the well-being of cattle, which are vital to the agricultural community. With the generous support of donors and the hard work of volunteers, Upeksha was able to provide substantial feed supplies to numerous farmers, helping to sustain their cattle and ease their burdens. This initiative not only addressed immediate needs but also demonstrated the community's commitment to supporting its farmers and fostering agricultural resilience.
